Hello, I'm Diego!

Who am I?

As I mentioned in the introductory video, I am a first-generation college student at the University of Washington. I am currecntly studying Informatics with a focus on HCI (Human-Computer Interaction)!

My Background:

I come from a small rural town in Cantral/Eastern Washington. Due to there not being many resources to exlpore ones interest, I am able to make the most with what I'm given even when given very little! Leaving my small hometown has allowed me to learn about my strengths as a minority and a person of color.

Why UX?

Originally I thought I would be going into the mechanical engineering field, well... I was wrong. I genuinely enjoy working with not only people but with technology, helping with the process of using technology for the greater good. As a UX designer, I get the best of both worlds along with the aspect of beign able to be creative!

What I Aim To Do As A Designer:

  • Help break practices in place that overlook marginalzed users.
  • Be a compassionate + empathetic designer.
  • Work to perfect my craft by continously learning.
